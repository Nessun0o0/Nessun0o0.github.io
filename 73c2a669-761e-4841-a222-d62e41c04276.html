## Impress the Gods!

Jotun is a hand-drawn action-exploration game set in Norse mythology. In Jotun, you play Thora, a Viking warrior who died an inglorious death and must prove herself to the Gods to enter Valhalla.

![Jotun Image](https://cdn2.unrealengine.com/Diesel%2Fproductv2%2Fjotun%2Fhome%2FEGS_ThunderLotus_JotunValhallaEdition_G2_02-1280x720-5570bcdbfb5a6459c2ad65e949160e12a1fbe59f.jpg)

## Meet the Jotun!

Explore vast regions of Norse Purgatory to find runes to unleash the jotun, giant Norse elementals.

![Jotun Image](https://cdn2.unrealengine.com/Diesel%2Fproductv2%2Fjotun%2Fhome%2FEGS_ThunderLotus_JotunValhallaEdition_G2_04-1280x720-147ba059274b53cd172bec980fb2de9b1f839c99.jpg)

## Intense boss rush action!

Fight them using only your massive two-handed axe, the blessings of the Gods and your skills!

![Jotun Image](https://cdn2.unrealengine.com/Diesel%2Fproductv2%2Fjotun%2Fhome%2FEGS_ThunderLotus_JotunValhallaEdition_G2_06-1280x720-642aac906125a7f690e26c7f34b81330bbd7011a.jpg)

## Test your skills in Valhalla Mode!

Jotun: Valhalla Edition features Valhalla Mode, the ultimate battle against even fiercer versions of the Jotun! A true challenge for those who wish to impress the Gods!
